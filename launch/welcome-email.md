# Welcome Email — Draft

Sent to each member after their OJS account is created during bulk sync. Single purpose: get them to set their password.

---

**Subject:** Set up your journal access — Existential Analysis

**From:** Society for Existential Analysis <[SEA email address]>

---

Hi [first name],

Your SEA membership now includes online access to *Existential Analysis*.

We've set up an account for you on our journal site using your email address **[email]**. To get started, you just need to set a password.

**[Set your password →]**

This link expires in 7 days. If it's expired, go to [OJS login URL] and click "Forgot your password?"

Once you've set your password, you can browse and read articles at:
[OJS journal URL]

If you have any questions, just reply to this email.

Best wishes,
The SEA Team

---

## Notes for SEA staff

- The password link is a one-time reset token generated by OJS
- If a member doesn't receive it: check spam, then re-send via `wp sea-ojs send-welcome-emails --user=<email>`
- The email address shown is their WP membership email — if they want to use a different one on OJS, they need to update it on the membership site first
